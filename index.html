About

I'm an undergraduate student studying Engineering Science at the University of Toronto, with a major in Robotics and a minor in Bioengineering.
My passion and interests lie at the intersection of technology and human health. I am particularly curious about genomics and its potential in the healthcare landscape.
In the past, I've worked on projects related to these interests, such as developing a computational platform to analyze toxiogenomic data, and inducing gene kockouts with CRISPR-Cas9.
Currently, I am working on creating an epigenetic clock using cell-free MeDIP data.

I am always looking for opportunities to improve my skills/pick up new ones -- whether it be in software development, statistical modelling, biotechnology, research, etc!
Reach out if you'd like to talk, I'd be happy to chat! :)
